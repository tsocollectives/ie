The Artistic Vision

Meet the Artisan & Founder

Welcome to my studio. I am the founder, designer, and artisan behind TSO Collectives. From my workspace in Limerick, Ireland, I dedicate hours to cleaning, detailing, painting, and baking glassware, as well as curing real floral resins.

I believe that glassware should not be generic or mass-produced. The cups we drink from during family dinners or the jewelry we wear when stepping out should carry human warmth, time, and detail. By painting botanical motifs and preserving nature's shamrocks and wildflowers, I bring unique, functional luxury into your daily rituals.

"My creations are not identical twin copies. Each brushstroke, leaf contour, and floral arrangement is unique, crafted exclusively for those who cherish authentic, slow-made art."

— Founder & Artisan, TSO Collectives
Meticulous Handcrafting

The Creation Process

Each product undergoes hours of preparation, detailing, and finish-hardening to ensure it remains a durable, timeless heirloom in your home.

01

Concept & Composition

Developing botanical layouts inspired by the wild Irish meadows and amber sunsets. Drafting detailed placements that wrap around the complex 3D contours of crystal goblets or metallic jewelry bezels.

02

Microscopic Painting

Using ultra-fine liner brushes to apply specialized Pebeo Vitrea 160 glossy glass paint. Outlining Shamrock emblems in pure gold enamels and applying vibrant floral layers with precision.

03

Vitreous Heat-Curing

Placing the dry, painted glassware into controlled domestic kilns. Curing them at high temperatures so the enamel layers crosslink and fuse directly with the glass surface, ensuring durability.

04

Resin Cast & Polish

Pouring crystal-clear, non-yellowing eco-resin under vacuum chambers to eliminate bubbles. Pressing real dried Irish wildflowers and shamrocks, ultraviolet-chamber curing, and hand-polishing.

Quality Assured & Insured Shipping

Glassware uses non-toxic, heat-cured Vitrea 160 paints. Jewelry uses hypoallergenic sterling silver or stainless steel bases. Everything is double-boxed with heavy bubble wrap to guarantee safe arrival at your door.
