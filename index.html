Artisanal Craftsmanship

The Creation Process

Every creation is born from a patient, rigorous cycle of design, execution, and finishing. Here is how we build luxury items that stand the test of time.

01

Design & Sketch

Developing concepts inspired by nature, floral structures, and elegant patterns. Planning layouts carefully for 3D curvatures of glass vessels.

02

Precision Painting

Applying high-grade acrylic enamel pigments manually under magnification. Adding metallic gold and silver detailing layer by layer.

03

Thermal Curing

Baking painted glassware in kiln controllers to melt and permanently cross-link paints to glass surfaces, ensuring extreme durability and water resistance.

04

Resin Curing & Polish

For jewelry, pouring eco-resins, vacuum-degassing bubbles, embedding flora, curing under UV chambers, and hand-polishing to a high-mirror crystal shine.
